FAQ

Why do I need to register?

Without registering, this web site would have no way of saving your measurement data, forcing you to start from scratch each time you visit. It is necessary for the operation of the site.

Who can see my information?

Your measurement information is only visible to you. You can make your profile public, but it's set to private by default.
